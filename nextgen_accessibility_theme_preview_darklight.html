Quick accessibility checks

  1. Ensure hero text is overlaid when using gradients.
  2. All primary CTAs have contrast â‰¥ 4.5:1 against their background.
  3. Keyboard focus visible (try tabbing) â€” primary has custom focus.
  4. Images have alt text or are marked aria-hidden when decorative.
  5. Use semantic headings and landmarks (header, main, aside).

Authoring tips

  • Write concise link text (avoid "click here").
  • Provide captions/alt text for investor documents and PDFs.
  • Maintain consistent focus order in interactive flows (forms).
  • Offer a visible high-contrast toggle for users who need stronger contrast.
